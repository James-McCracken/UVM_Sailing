A letter to our Alumni and Friends:

The University of Vermont Sailing Team is so excited to start another excellent season this year, along with Quentin Chafee '11 as our new head coach! We’ve started to shake off the rust and get back into college sailing, with our new fleet of FJ's. At practice, we have been able to get about 12-15 boats out on the water, thanks to the addition of a large first year class. Luckily, events do not start until September 13th, which gives us plenty of time to prepare and start the season off strong. We've got a promising season ahead of us, so cheer us on and follow the results on TechScore!

SAVE THE DATE ALERT:

Our Alumni/Fundraiser Banquet will be on Parent's weekend this year- weekend of October 11th. So be sure to put that in your calendar and book hotels ASAP. We’re starting to prepare what is always an incredible and successful night, so you should too! The Lake Champlain Community Sailing Center and Women in the Wind are also hosting a breakfast for us before the Alumni Regatta to present plans for the new sailing facilities!
