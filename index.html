A letter to our Alumni and Friends

The University of Vermont Sailing Team is so excited to start another excellent season this year! With our new fleet of FJ’s we’ve started to shake off the rust and get back into college sailing! Recently we’ve been able to get 14 boats out on the water for practice, and will continue to do so with the addition of 14 new first years to the team! We are also lucky that events don’t start until September 14th, giving us plenty of time to get ready to kick some butt. We’ve got some promising first years, a new fleet, and a goal of making top 10 in the nation this year! Get ready to see us climb!
